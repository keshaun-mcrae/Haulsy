# Trust & Safety Reference

## Scam patterns (warning copy)
- “Can we move to WhatsApp/text immediately?”
- Requests for deposits or upfront payment
- “My cousin will pick it up” / weird logistics
- Refuses public meetup or tries to rush you
- Price is unrealistically low for the item

## Meetup tips (short copy)
- Meet in a public place
- Prefer daytime
- Bring a friend if possible
- Confirm item condition before exchanging money

## Report reasons
- Scam / suspicious
- Harassment
- Prohibited item
- Fake listing
- Spam
